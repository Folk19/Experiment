Tombstone Territorial Park<!-- -->
You know when the Yukon government advises you to leave a hiking plan with local police before traveling into the Tombstone Park area you're in for a wild time. This 2,200-square-kilometer (849 square miles) park, located 90 minutes from Dawson City, is filled with rugged peaks, permafrost landforms and wildlife. <!-- -->
More info: Env.gov.yk.ca
